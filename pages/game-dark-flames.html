Dark Flames

Darkness has conquered everything. You must venture into the depths of the Forest of Sorrow in search of Cinders - the last fragments of the Primordial Flame, and your only hope to rekindle the light and restore warmth to a dying world.

The path ahead is ever unknown. You will risk both body and mind. With you, only a faint torch. A roll of the dice will determine your fate.

Dark Flames is a simple game developed by a two-person team in seven days for the 1-BIT JAM 2023 and released on itch.io as a free play-in-browser game.

Made with Unity and C#.

I coded everything except the shader (that looks very cool, I know.)

Notable features

  • Use of Scriptable Objects for data management
  • At the start of each run, a unique hero is randomly generated with a name and stats
  • Every step along the way is a “room” that gets instantiated as the characters goes forward, and every run is a pseudorandom sequence of rooms

One thing I’m pretty proud of

I programmed a dice roll mechanic, with its 2D animation. The dice determines outcomes such as damage, resources, or special events.

It’s actually just a 2D image of the dice faces, masked, and smoothly moved until it reaches the desired face, previously calculated. The image position had to be scaled based on the current resolution, otherwise the animation would break whenever the user resized the window.
